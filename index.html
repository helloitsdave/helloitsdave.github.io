Neal Martin Hughes

Neal Martin Hughes is an Irish artist, singer, songwriter, musician and film-maker originally from Dungannon, Northern Ireland.

Previously Neal worked as an Arts researcher at the BBC before returning to college to finish a BSC in Social Sciences in 2014. He then went on to study an MA degree at the Irish World Academy of Music and Dance at the University of Limerick, graduating in 2017.

Neal currently works as a Music Therapist based in Belfast.

This site compiles samples of Neal's various artistic works.

Driving By Night - 2003/2009

Neal fronted this 5-piece band from Cookstown, signed to Universal Music Publishing.

They performed at the prestigious In The City festival in Manchester and in South By Southwest (SXSW) in Austin, Texas.

They were produced by Rupert Christie (U2/Greenday) and supported many high profile acts including Keane, The Thrills, Kings of Leon, Faithless, and Arctic Monkeys.
